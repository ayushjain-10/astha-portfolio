Coding Philosophy

Just like choreography, good code tells a story. It has rhythm, structure, and purpose. Every function, every variable is a movement in a larger dance of logic and creativity.

I approach coding the same way I approach dance - with curiosity, patience, and a willingness to experiment until the pieces fall into place.
