Allure

Allure is a contemporary dance work that brings traditional Indian elements into conversation with modern movement practices. The piece acts as a bridge between Indian cultural roots and contemporary thinking, with a strong emphasis on musicality to shape both movement and emotional rhythm. Lighting is used intentionally to reflect the dancers internal experiences, allowing the audience to feel the moment rather than simply observe it. Conceptually, Allure explores femininity and the evolving understanding of beauty. While earlier notions of beauty were often rooted in external appearance, the work traces a shift toward inner awareness and emotional depth. 02

Delusions

Delusions is a contemporary dance work that examines the tension between perception and reality. The piece explores how internal narratives, expectations, and emotional states shape the way individuals experience the world around them. Through layered movement and shifting dynamics, the choreography reflects moments of clarity, distortion, and fragmentation. The dancers navigate cycles of control and release, revealing how delusions can both protect and confine the self. By emphasizing contrast in energy, rhythm, and spatial relationships, Delusions invites the audience to question what is real, what is imagined, and how easily the two begin to blur.

Style Hip-Hop / Contemporary
