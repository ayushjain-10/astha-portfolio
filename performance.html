About This Performance

Nayi

As a performer in "Nayi", I engage with the idea of transformation and becoming through the body. The work explores the meaning of “nayi” as newness and renewal, and my role within the choreography requires me to move between familiarity and change. Through repetition, resistance, and release, I experience how transformation is physically negotiated rather than simply represented. The movement language draws from contemporary techniques while remaining informed by cultural memory. As a performer, I navigate this intersection by embodying gestures that carry history while allowing them to evolve into new forms. This process challenges me to remain present, adaptable, and responsive, emphasizing internal awareness over fixed aesthetics.

This piece showcases a blend of contemporary techniques with elements of classical and modern dance, creating a unique visual experience that resonates with viewers on a profound level.

About This Performance

Blaze

As a performer in Blaze, I engage with intensity, momentum, and sustained energy as core physical and emotional drivers of the work. The choreography demands a heightened state of presence, requiring me to navigate speed, force, and control while remaining responsive to the ensemble. Through this process, I explore how energy is generated, contained, and released within the body. The work emphasizes dynamic shifts and continuous motion, challenging me to balance precision with instinct. As a performer, I experience Blaze as an investigation of endurance and transformation, where physical exertion becomes a tool for accessing emotional clarity and resilience. The movement requires both strength and vulnerability, allowing intensity to function as a form of expression rather than spectacle.

This piece showcases a blend of contemporary techniques with elements of classical and modern dance, creating a unique visual experience that resonates with viewers on a profound level.
